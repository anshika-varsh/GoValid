📚 Startup Goals Guide

Business Goal/Vision: Describe your long-term vision for the company. What do you want to achieve in 5-10 years?

Monetization Strategy: Explain how you plan to make money. Consider subscription models, one-time purchases, advertising, etc.

Social Impact: What positive change will your startup bring to society? How will it make the world better?

Timeline & Expectations: Set realistic short-term (6 months) and long-term (2-5 years) goals and milestones.
